Miayjima

Miyajima is the most common name given to the island of Itsukushima, located in Hiroshima Bay and overlooking the Seto Inland Sea. Classified as one of the three most beautiful sites in Japan thanks in particular to its floating torii and Shinto shrine, this sacred island is a tourist hotspot in the archipelago.
