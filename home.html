Discover our travel guide to Japan!

Paradoxical and intoxicating, Japan plunges the traveler into the heart of an ocean of dazzling contrasts. From the fervor of Tokyo to the assertive authenticity of Kyoto, its poetic countryside, its majestic mountains and its lake landscapes offer the traveler an incredible variety of activities and atmospheres.

Discover our destination

Japan is not only visited through its cities. It is also discovered in its countryside, its mountains and its plains and through all the diversity of the geography of Japan. Your Japanese trip is an invitation to immerse yourself in the nature that surrounds the archipelago. Its fauna and flora are all reasons to get away from the tumultuous cities, where tradition and modernity coexist. The poetic landscapes of Japan give you another facet of the country, far from the usual tourist sites.
